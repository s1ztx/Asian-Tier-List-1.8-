Community Guidelines

The Rulebook

Everything that keeps testing fair, tiers accurate, and the community safe. Read this in full before applying for a rank test.

General Conduct Testing Rules Tier Integrity Discord Conduct Appeals & Retests Punishments
📋
SECTION 01

General Conduct

  • Respect everyone. Harassment, hate speech, and targeted insults toward staff, testers, or members are never allowed.
  • No cheating. Any client modification that grants an unfair combat advantage — reach, kill-aura, auto-clickers with pattern automation, hitboxes, or similar — results in an immediate tier ban.
  • One account per player. Testing on alternate accounts to bypass a punishment or manipulate placement is prohibited.
  • No account sharing. Your tier is tied to your skill. Letting someone else test on your behalf voids the result.
  • Follow staff instructions. Testers and moderators have final say during a test session — arguing mid-test can end it early.
🧪
SECTION 02

Testing Rules

  • Book through the correct channel. Tests are only valid when scheduled and conducted by an official tester listed on the Testers page.
  • Pick your gamemode. You may only be tested in a gamemode you have selected in advance — BedWars, Fireball Fight, Sumo, Nodebuff, Boxing, BUHC, or Bridges.
  • Stable connection required. Excessive lag or disconnects during a test may void the result at the tester's discretion.
  • No coaching mid-test. Friends or spectators may not call out opponent movement, cooldowns, or strategy during your test.
  • Recording is mandatory. All tests are recorded by the tester for review and dispute purposes.
  • Cooldown between attempts. A failed test requires a minimum wait period before a retest can be booked — see Appeals & Retests below.
NOTE Your tier is gamemode-specific. A Sumo tier does not carry over to Bridges or BedWars.
⬡
SECTION 03

Tier Integrity

  • Fourteen ranks, five tiers. The ladder runs D-, D, D+, C-, C, C+, B-, B, B+, A-, A, A+, S-, S from lowest to highest.
  • Tiers are earned, not gifted. No rank may be assigned without a recorded test, regardless of reputation or prior results elsewhere.
  • Public leaderboard. Every ranked player's current tier is visible on the Leaderboards page — tier changes are logged, not silently edited.
  • Boosting is banned. Deliberately losing to inflate an opponent's result, or arranging a scripted outcome, voids both players' tiers.
  • Inactivity decay. Tiers left untested for an extended period may be flagged for a mandatory retest to stay on the public leaderboard.
💬
SECTION 04

Discord Conduct

  • Keep channels on-topic. Use the correct channel for test requests, tier disputes, and general chat.
  • No self-promotion. Advertising other servers, Discords, or channels without staff approval is not permitted.
  • NSFW and illegal content is banned outright, with zero exceptions and no warning period.
  • Impersonation is not tolerated. Do not imitate staff, testers, or other members, in name or avatar.
  • Ticket etiquette. Open one ticket per issue and wait for a response before pinging staff again.
🔁
SECTION 05

Appeals & Retests

  • Disputing a result. Open a ticket within 48 hours of your test with the recording reference if you believe the outcome was incorrect.
  • Retest cooldown. A standard failed test requires a wait period before rebooking; repeated fast rebooking may be denied by staff.
  • Ban appeals. Players punished for cheating may appeal once, no earlier than the period stated in their punishment notice.
  • Staff decisions are final once an appeal has been reviewed by two or more staff members.
⚠️
SECTION 06

Punishments

Low

Off-topic spam, minor disrespect, or a first-time testing rule slip. Typically a verbal warning or short mute.

Mid

Repeated warnings, boosting attempts, or coaching during a test. Results in a tier reset and a temporary testing ban.

High

Cheating, account sharing, or targeted harassment. Results in a permanent tier ban and Discord ban with no appeal window.
